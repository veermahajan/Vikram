Oil, Gas, and Coal - a Poem by Vikram

OIL, GAS AND COAL

Oil, gas and coal,
Have taken their toll.

Oil and coal are the source of our energy,
And have been so for multiple centuries.

However, all three are examples of greenhouse gas,
And are destroying our environment en masse.

They release the greenhouse gases of carbon dioxide and methane,
And what these greenhouses gases do is dangerous and inhumane,

Inside our atmosphere, they trap heat,
If you want to hear more, just take a seat!

For when the heat is trapped,
From the coal that we extract,

Icebergs in the ocean will melt,
And their impact will be felt,

For the temperatures will rise,
And the oceans will rise,

Leading to the demise
Of coastal communities

And no one will have immunity
There will be global warming,
And more storms will come a-storming,


Over 800 million people will face the impact,
Of all the coal that we sought to extract,


By 2050, orangutans, gorillas, vaquitas,
Polar bears, rhinos and cheetahs,

Will all be extinct,
Before we have time to so much as blink!

This all can still be fixed,
But many politicians are intermixed,

With the coal and oil industry,
These industries rule the country.

Some senators and representatives
Are owned by oil executives.

Bills to limit oil and coal come one after another,
But these senators block and filibuster!

The water in Flint, Michigan, is poisoned,
A 'harmless' fuel that is deadlier than a gun!
